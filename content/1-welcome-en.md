# Voboost Installer

We are about to install the Voboost application onto your vehicle. This software significantly expands your multimedia system's capabilities.

Voboost does not permanently alter immutable partitions. A factory reset of the vehicle's multimedia system will clear these modifications.

This installer will guide you through the process of setting up Voboost on your Voyah multimedia system.

You can read more details about the features at<br>
[voboost.ru/features](https://voboost.ru/features).
